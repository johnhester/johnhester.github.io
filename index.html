About Me
My story

Before diving into software development I gathered experience across multiple industries ranging from customer service, to construction, to logistics, to life-guarding & life-guard instruction, and even a short stint as a Bellman. This diverse work history has given me a wealth of experience along with the ability to function at a high level with coworkers and teams with diverse backgrounds and skill-sets.

When working in multiple industries one develops the ability to problem solve from many different angles. Most people approach problems based on many factors including how they were raised, their schooling, chosen profession, and coworkers. Even their personal outlook on life has an effect on how an individual approaches a problem

Having spent time not only in different jobs, but in wildly different industries I have been exposed to a variety of thought processes and problem solving patterns. Whether it's assisting a customer through dealing with multiple departments in a call center, laying gas lines under a house, or tracking orders for a construction site, every experience has a lesson or skill that can lead to growth, and I believe this gives me a distinct advantage stepping into the world of software development.
